Our Workflow

Step 1 (Investigation)
Requirement Hearing: Go through customers requirements to identify needs.
Estimation: Create technical/scheduled proposals based on requirements.

Step 2 (Estimation)
Planning: Investigate & analyze business logic, create project plan & delivery milestones.
Designing: Designing application architecture as well as detail screen.

Step 3 (Deployment)
Coding & Testing: Coding & testing based on design results.

Step 4 (Delivery & Maintenance)
Delivery: Source code delivery, includes, all design & investigation documents. We also support the deployment of the product
Maintenance: Create technical/schedules proposals based on requirements
